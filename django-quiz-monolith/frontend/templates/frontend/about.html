About Quiz App

Empowering education through interactive learning

Our Mission

Quiz App is designed to bridge the gap between traditional learning and modern technology. We provide an intuitive platform that enables teachers to create engaging quizzes and students to test their knowledge in an interactive environment.

Key Features

For Students

  • Interactive quiz taking experience
  • Real-time feedback and scoring
  • Progress tracking and analytics
  • Personal dashboard with insights

For Teachers

  • Easy quiz creation and management
  • Student performance monitoring
  • Detailed analytics and reports
  • Class and student management
